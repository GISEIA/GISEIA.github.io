Established since 1999, Hi-Target is the first professional high-precision surveying and mapping instrument brand to be successfully listed in China. Hi-Target provides a wide range of surveying equipment including GNSS receivers, CORS stations, Total Station, 3D Laser Scanners, GIS Data Collectors, UAV/UAS, and Hydrographic products to offer complete commercial solutions for various industries.

As a leading brand in the geospatial industry, Hi-Target invests heavily in research and development, and collaborates with more than 100 universities globally to bring the latest positioning technology and innovation for product development. For over 20 years, Hi-Target has approximately 2,500 employees worldwide, with an established network of 28 subsidiaries, 28 branches and more than 200 partners in over 60 countries to service and support our customers.
